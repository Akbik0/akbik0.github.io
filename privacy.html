Privacy and Cookies Policy

1. Overview
This Privacy and Cookies Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information obtained from users of this website. By accessing or using this website, you consent to the practices described in this Policy.

2. Use of Cookies
Cookies are small data files stored on your device that assist in providing a better browsing experience. This website utilizes cookies to enhance functionality, analyze site traffic, and remember user preferences. Cookies may be set by us (first-party cookies) or by third-party services integrated into our website.

3. Categories of Cookies
The cookies used on this website fall into the following categories:

Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for the operation of the site. Without these, the website may not function properly.

Analytical/Performance Cookies: Allow us to recognize and count visitors and understand how they move around the site. This helps improve performance.

Functionality Cookies: Enable the website to remember your preferences (such as language or region).

Targeting Cookies: May be used to deliver advertisements relevant to you and your interests. 

4. Third-Party Cookies
We may employ third-party services, such as analytics providers, which may set cookies on your device. These third parties are responsible for their own privacy practices, and we encourage users to review their respective policies.

5. Cookie Consent and Control
Upon your first visit, a notice will inform you about the use of cookies. You have the right to accept or decline non-essential cookies. Most browsers allow you to manage your cookie preferences through their settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may impact site functionality.

6. Data Collection and Use
We may collect non-personally identifiable information through cookies to monitor user behavior and improve the website. No personally identifiable data (such as names, addresses, or contact information) is collected through cookies unless explicitly provided by the user.

7. Changes to This Policy
We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy and Cookies Policy at any time. Any changes will take effect immediately upon posting the revised policy to the website. It is the user's responsibility to review this page periodically.
