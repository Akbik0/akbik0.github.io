Privacy and Cookies Policy

1. Overview
This Privacy and Cookies Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information from users of this website. By using this site, you agree to the practices described below.

2. Use of Cookies
Cookies are small files stored on your device that help improve your experience. We use cookies to support website functions, analyze traffic, and remember user preferences. These cookies can be first-party (set by us) or third-party (set by services we use).

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies: Required for basic site functions. Without these, the site may not work properly.

Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve its design and performance.

Functionality Cookies: Remember choices you make, like language or region.

Targeting Cookies: Used to show ads that match your interests.

4. Third-Party Cookies
Some third-party tools (like analytics services) may place their own cookies on your device. These services follow their own privacy rules, and we recommend reviewing their policies.

5. Cookie Consent and Control
When you first visit the site, you’ll see a notice about our cookie use. You can choose to accept or reject non-essential cookies. You can also change your cookie settings anytime in your browser. Keep in mind, disabling cookies may affect how some parts of the site work.

6. Data Collection and Use
We may use cookies to collect non-personal information (such as browsing behavior) to help us improve the site. We do not collect personal information like your name or contact details unless you provide them directly.

8. Changes to This Policy
